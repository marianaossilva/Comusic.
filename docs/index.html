Our Methodology

Network Analysis

Using data from Billboard and Spotify, we construct a collaborative success-based network and apply tools of network science to study its structure.

Collaboration Profiles

By means of topological metrics, we define four categories of collaboration profiles, each with two levels of subcategories. The classes are defined according to the specific social metrics' features.

Clustering

Applying a clustering algorithm, we identify three well-defined communities with distinct collaboration patterns and notable discrepancies in levels of musical success.

Statistical Analysis

Then, we conduct a statistical correlation analysis to evaluate the association between collaboration profiles and the artist's success.
