Collaboration Profiles

In order to define and (subsequently) identify collaboration profiles, we consider well known metrics dependent on the node (Clustering Coefficient, Eigenvector, Degree and Weighted Degree) and related to the whole graph (Closeness, Eccentricity and Betweenness). From these metrics, we define four categories of collaboration profiles, each with two levels of subcategories.

Categories

Interaction (Degree and Weighted Degree)

Single Artists Collaboration

Multi-artists Collaboration
